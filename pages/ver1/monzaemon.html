Monzaemon

  • Stage: Perfect
  • Type: Vaccine
  • Sleep Time: 11:00 PM
  • Power: 107

A Digimon shrouded in mystery.

Monzaemon looks like an ordinary stuffed bear, but rumor has it that someone—or something—has climbed in through the zipper on its back.

It wraps up its opponent in the love overflowing from this cute body (albeit with scary eyes), and makes them feel happy. It uses its special move Hearts Attack to flings hearts at the enemy.
