Birdramon

  • Stage: Adult
  • Type: Vaccine
  • Sleep Time: 11:00 PM
  • Power: 55

A Giant Bird Digimon wreathed in blazing flames.

Like Meramon, Birdramon is a Digimon formed from the Internet’s defensive Firewall. Birdramon flies through the sky using its massive wings.

Despite not having any warlike qualities, Birdramon will launch a frenzied counterattack in the face of enemy assaults.

Birdramon’s special move is Meteor Wing, by which it flaps its wings to send feathers shooting down like meteors.
